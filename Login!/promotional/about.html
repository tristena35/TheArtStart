The Origins Of Artivational Studios

We all go back to freshmen year, when we all enrolled in the Computer Science Program.We all knew each other, but it wasn't until Junior Year that we all got closer.It was AP Computer Science that really brought us to our fullest extent.

After we were done with Junior Year, our teacher wanted us to create products that could help future generations. We all pitched in our ideas, but the idea that stood out the most was Tristen's. His idea was based on helping artists to get their work exposed.

After we had heard his idea, all of us came together and agreed to work as a team and we are known today as Artivational Studios. Our mission is to bring more opportunities out there for people in the Art Industry and we hope our future innovations will expose all artists out there.
