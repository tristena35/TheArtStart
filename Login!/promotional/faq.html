Who is Artivational Studios?

Artivational Studios is the underdog and is ready to knock out the top dog companies.

What is ArtStart?

ArtStart is a website where you can share art to other fellow artists. With a simple upload, youre art will be displayed on your profile on the site. This website is like a social media website as well. You can communicate with fellow artist to either compliment on their work or just give them feedback for improvement.

When will the website be available?

We are working very hard to get the website up and running as soon as possible. Our goal is to get the ArtStart started at the end of Feburary. We are making rapid progress as of right now and we will be sure to please all our customers.

What kind of art can you upload to ArtStart?

ArtStart will allow you to upload any kind of art on our website. Currently, we are focusing on allowing the user to upload picture-related art or paintings. We want to futher expand this by allowing user to upload film as well. We want to user to be exposed to all the kinds of art that is out there in the world.

How did the idea of ArtStart come up?

Our leader, Tristen Aguilar has created the idea of ArtStart.Originally, we were going to make a website called PhotoMoto. A website where you can upload pictures (kind of like instagram). Later on, Tristen further expanded the idea and it is known today as ArtStart.

How long have you guys been working on ArtStart?

Once we all decided on the idea of ArtStart, we got started right away and got a little too excited and put ourselves in a little mess. The exact date that we started the website was in the beginning of November, but we really got to work on December.

Where was Artivational Studios before they formed?

All of our members were all in the same high school and program in our school as well. We all had different motives in terms of programming and we started to doubt on what we wanted to do in the future. It was not until our senior year is when we all got together and decided to create a website.
