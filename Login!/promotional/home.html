Welcome to

Objective

Artivational Studios is excited to be working on ArtStart. A website where artists can share their works to millions of artists around the world. Our objective is to allow the chance for people to exploit their artwork because many don't get the chance to show how talented they are.

The Future

In the future, Artivational Studios is hoping to release ArtStart by the end of February.After it has been released, we are willing to take any feedback from our users. With the feedback that we receive, we will further expand on our website to make it as the user wants it.

Easy to Use

ArtStart is user friendly, so anyone can use it. On this website site, you're allowed to upload your finest works to share all over the internet. You can as well check out our Explore page to see other people's works from many different kinds of categories.

ArtStart Features

On ArtStart, you will be allowed to:

  • Create an account to connect with fellow artists
  • Upload your art work for others to see
  • Keep track of your favorite art on the site
  • Comment on people's work to help them improve
  • Explore People's work from many different categories

All these features are here to help improve your use of this website. We want to make sure that your art is uploaded to ArtStart
